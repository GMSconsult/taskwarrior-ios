# Privacy Policy for Taskwarrior
 
**Last updated: April 21, 2025**
 
## Overview
 
Taskwarrior is a task management app built for privacy-conscious users. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.
 
## Data Collection
 
Taskwarrior collects no data. We have no servers, no analytics, no crash reporting, and no advertising.
 
## Local Storage
 
All tasks and app data are stored locally on your device. We have no access to this data.
 
## Sync Server
 
If you choose to configure a third-party sync server, your data is transmitted directly between your device and that server. We have no involvement in or access to that connection. Please refer to your sync server provider's privacy policy for information on how they handle your data.
 
## Third Party Services
 
Taskwarrior does not integrate with any third party services or SDKs.
 
## Contact
 
If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on our GitHub repository.
 
## Changes
 
We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page.
